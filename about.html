Who I am

I’m Mohamed Sharfuddeen Nadeem, an IT & Cybersecurity professional based in Bruges, Belgium. I study Applied Computer Science (Cybersecurity track) at Howest, and I’ve built my skills through hands-on labs, certifications, and real-world projects.

What I do

I focus on securing infrastructure: internal network reconnaissance, vulnerability validation, hardening recommendations, and making security findings understandable for teams who need to act on them. I enjoy building repeatable workflows and automation (Python/Bash) to reduce manual work.

What I’m looking for

I’m aiming for roles where I can combine security with systems/network engineering — such as Junior Security Engineer, SOC/Blue Team, Network/Security Engineer, or IT Security Consultant — where communication and practical execution matter.
