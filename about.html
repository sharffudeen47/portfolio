The Person Behind the Portfolio

My Journey

I’m Mohamed Sharfuddeen Nadeem. Based in Bruges, I’ve spent the last few years at Howest (Applied Computer Science) diving deep into how systems are built and how they can be better protected. I believe that true security isn't a bolt-on feature; it’s a mindset built on technical excellence.

My Focus

I specialize in the "internal" side of security infrastructure. From network reconnaissance to vulnerability validation and system hardening, I focus on turning technical findings into actionable strategies. I’m a big advocate for automation (Python/Bash) to ensure that security is consistent and repeatable.

My Goal

I’m aiming for roles where engineering and security meet. Whether it’s as a Junior Security Engineer, part of a SOC/Blue Team, or as a Security Consultant, I want to work in environments where practical execution and clear communication are valued as much as technical skill.
